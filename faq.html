FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q. What are the Daily Value percentages based on?
    A. They are based on a 2,000 calorie diet. Using standard goverment recommendations for nutrition.
  • Q. How did you get those prices?
    A. I went on local grocery stores websites and very rarely amazon to get prices.
  • Q. How many foods are in the tables?
    A. 175 foods of the same foods in each table, more will be added in the future
  • Q. Why cups, grams, kcals, liters and ounces?
    A. I wanted to have rankings by volume, weight and calories, I also wanted liters and ounces for people who don't use cups or grams.
  • Q. How did you get the data?
    A. I made a list of foods and webscraped Cronometer, I then cleaned the data to my liking.
  • Q. Why are there N/A values?
    A. If it is a percentage, the source didn't have the data. If it is a price, I couldn't convert to the unit automatically. I did manage to get all the cup prices which could convert to liters, but I had to sometimes manually get the data.
  • Q. Is the price data up to date?
    A. No, the price data is from December, 2022.
